Hello! I'm a CS PhD student in the Harvard Slade Lab, supported by the NSF GRFP. My goal is to use computer vision and human-robot interaction to create useful assistive devices.

I'm currently working on a smartphone-based navigation assistant for people who are blind or low-vision.
